About

An app built with
love, for Muslims.

Hikmati (حكمتي , "My Wisdom") is an independent Islamic app currently in development, built by a Muslim developer who wanted a modern, thoughtful way to engage with the Quran and Sunnah daily.

The app is powered by React Native, uses verified Islamic content sources (Quran.com API, Sunnah.com), and incorporates AI to help users connect with relevant wisdom based on their personal state and intentions.

We are preparing to launch on both iOS (App Store) and Android (Google Play). All content has been curated with care for accuracy and authenticity.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

1. Information We Collect

Hikmati collects minimal personal information. When you sign up for our pre-launch mailing list, we collect your email address solely to notify you of the app's launch.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To send you launch notifications and updates about Hikmati.
  • To improve the app experience based on aggregate, anonymized usage data.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage & Security

Email addresses collected via our pre-launch form are stored securely. The Hikmati app stores bookmarks and preferences locally on your device using AsyncStorage and does not transmit personal data to our servers.

4. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party APIs to provide content:

  • Quran.com API , for Quranic verses and translations.
  • Sunnah.com API , for authenticated Hadiths.
  • Anthropic Claude API , for AI-powered Journey generation. No personally identifiable information is sent; only the user-provided text prompt is transmitted.

5. Children's Privacy

Hikmati is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your email from our mailing list at any time by contacting us at hikmati.app@outlook.com.
